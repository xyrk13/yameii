Yameii Bot – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Yameii, a Discord bot operated by notpzyko, collects, uses, stores, and protects user data in compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

1. Who is Responsible for Your Data?

The data controller is the person who operates the bot:

  • Discord Contact: notpzyko
  • For all data-related inquiries, contact the bot owner via Discord.

2. What Data We Collect and Why

Data Type Purpose Legal Basis
User IDs, Message Content, Timestamps Economy, moderation, games, role management, chat models Legitimate Interests
User Audio Files (MP3, WAV, etc.) Transcription via Groq API Consent (by uploading audio)
User Nicknames & Roles Temporary nickname features, role management Legitimate Interests
Bot Currency Data (debt, credit, scores) Economy & credit score tracking Legitimate Interests
Avatar URLs & Webhooks Visual identity for webhooks and Markov replies Legitimate Interests

3. Use of Third Parties

Yameii uses the following third-party services:

  • Groq API: for audio transcription (groq.com)
  • UnbelievaBoat API: for user economy operations (unbelievaboat.com)

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Persistent data is stored locally (e.g., credits.json, message_log.db).
  • Temporary data (image messages, temporary nicknames) is purged after its use (5–15 minutes).
  • Audio files are processed by Groq but not stored locally.
  • Data is retained until manually deleted or if the bot is removed from a server.

5. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to certain types of processing
  • Request data portability (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact notpzyko on Discord.

6. Data Transfers Outside the UK

Some services may process data outside the UK (e.g., Groq, UnbelievaBoat). These services are expected to use appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

7. Children’s Data

Yameii is not intended for users under the age of 13. If data from a child is collected by mistake, notify server admins or the bot owner for removal.

8. Updates to This Policy

Changes to this policy will be posted on this page. Major updates may be announced via bot update channels.
